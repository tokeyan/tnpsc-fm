Indian Air Force

The Indian Air Force is the air force of the Indian Armed Forces. Its primary purpose is to protect Indian airspace and to conduct air warfare during armed conflict. It is led by a four-star Air Force commander known as the Air Chief Marshal. It consists of seven regiments.
Arjun Singh was the first and only officer to be promoted to the rank of five-star Marshal in the Indian Air Force.
