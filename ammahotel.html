Amma Hotel

Amma Restaurant is the first project in India in Tamil Nadu. It was started by Jayalalithaa. The plan was not even included in the election manifesto. This project was started by the mother of the leader of the revolution to satisfy the hunger of the poor and the common people. Then it became a pioneer project for India. The scheme is currently being implemented in Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan, West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh and Karnataka. Tamil Nadu is a pioneer for these states. Jayalalithaa is dependent on them
