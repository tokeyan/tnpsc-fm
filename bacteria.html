Bacteria

Bacteria are single-celled prokaryotes (nuclei). They are considered to be the first living creatures to appear on Earth. Type These are located. The study of bacteria is called 'bacteriology'. Bacteria range in size from 1um to 5um (micrometers) under the world of Monira in the catalog. These can be classified into two types based on respiration. They are:
1. Airborne bacteria (requires oxygen for respiration)
2. Anaerobic respiratory bacteria (does not require oxygen for respiration)
Bacteria are classified according to cell shape. They are:
Basil: Goal-shaped bacteria e.g. Bacillus anthracis spirilla: spiral-shaped bacteria e.g. Helicobacter pylori crow: spherical or ball-shaped bacteria. (Streptococcus) or clusters (Styphilococcus). They stick together in pairs (Diplococcus),: Comma-shaped bacteria e.g. Vibrio cholerae.
