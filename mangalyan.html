ISRO Mars Mission

The Mars Orbiter Mission, also known as Mangalyan, was launched by the Indian Space Research Organization on November 5, 2013. Joined Mars Orbit on September 24, 2014. India is proud to be the first Asian country to reach Mars with the Mangalyan victory. India is also the 4th country to enter the orbit of Mars. India is also proud to be the first country to successfully send a satellite to Mars in its first attempt.
