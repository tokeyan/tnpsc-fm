Virus

A virus is a very small particle made up of a genetic material and a protein. These are between the living and the non-living. In Latin, virus means 'poison'. Viruses are forced parasites that live inside the cell. The course on viruses is called 'Virology'. Viruses are 10,000 times smaller than bacteria. Viruses have different forms. They are, spherical, spherical or other shapes.
