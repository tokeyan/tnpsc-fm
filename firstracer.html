First Indian Racer N.Karthik

Born in Chennai and lives in Coimbatore, he was the first and only Indian to compete in the F1, considered one of the premier motor races in the world. He is currently representing Jordan in the annual F1 tournament. He was awarded the Padma Shri in 2010
