Right To Information

Right The Right to Information Act 2005 (RTI) gives every Indian citizen the right to know the information they need in public sector and government aided private companies. To this end, the Right to Information Bill was introduced in Parliament and passed in the Lok Sabha on 11 May 2005. The states passed it on May 12, 2005 and approved it on June 15, 2005.Empowering citizens to promote transparency and accountability in the functioning of government, to curb corruption and to make our democracy work for the people in its true sense - are fundamental objectives of the Right to Information Act.
