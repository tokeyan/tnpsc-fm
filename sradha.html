Dr Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan

celebrated as Teachers' Day to mark the birth anniversary of Dr Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, who was one of the most recognised and influential Indian thinkers in the field of academics. Dr Radhakrishnan was the first Vice-President and the second President of independent India- from 1962 to 1967. A renowned philosopher, statesman and teacher, he is remembered for introducing western philosophies into Indian society.
• Dr Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan was the first vice-president of India and the second President of the country.
• He was nominated multiple times for the Noble Peace Prize and was also graced with the Bharat Ratna Award.
• He was an educator, renowned author and a celebrated teacher who was highly regarded by his students.
