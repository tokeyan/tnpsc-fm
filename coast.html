Indian Coast Guard

The Indian Coast Guard was established in 1978 as the Independent Armed Forces of India under the Coast Guard Act, 1978 of the Indian Parliament. It operates under the Ministry of Defense. The Coast Guard cooperates with the Navy, Fisheries, Customs and Central and State Police.
