First Lithium Refinery Factory

India’s first Lithium refinery which will process Lithium ore to produce battery-grade material will be set up in Gujarat. Manikaran Power Limited, one of the country’s largest power trading and renewable energy companies will be investing over Rs 1,000 crore to set up this refinery. They are currently exploring a couple of places in Sanand and Dholera to set up this refinery.
